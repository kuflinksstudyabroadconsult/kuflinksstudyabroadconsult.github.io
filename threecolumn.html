1. Introduction

Kuflinks Study Abroad Consult(The company) provides educational Consulting Services to Clients(The Student) seeking study abroad opportunities at a non-refundable fee of two Million Naira(₦2,000,000) only. The Company carries out the following services to Client:

  • University/Program Selection based on Client's Choice and Career Objective
  • Documents Collection, Verification/CV Rewriting in the format required by individual university.
  • Statement of Purpose Writing
  • Motivation Letter writing
  • University Application for admission and documents uploading, application Status Monitoring and On-going Support
  • Contact Partners in respect of Funds (Tuition fees and Living expenses) for Client. Help Client to Source for Student Employment Opportunities, Scholarships and grants
  • Offer Visa Guidance Session and Support. Assessment of Visa Requirements and Eligibility. Advice on Financial Requirements and Proof of Funds. Provide Information on Health Insurance.
  • Offer Cultural Orientation Session, such as Fostering cultural awareness and understanding, enhancing adaptation to the host country's customs and norms, providing support for client development of essential skills for academics and personal success, and building a supportive community of international students
  • Provide guidance for enrollment, modify enrollment process, make available, the requirements and check list for enrollment. acquaint the client with the enrollment fees and deadlines
  • Career Counselling and development Support
  • Exploration of Career Options and industries by conducting a personality assessments, interest and skills set assessments, and career values and motivations assessments. Provide Insights into industry trends and outlook. Job Market analysis and Requirements
  • Career Transition Support and Reinvention coaching identifying transferable skills for new career. Explore new trending industries through research.
